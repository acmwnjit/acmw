News of ACM-W NJIT

ACM-W Former VP for MS student's, Vinolia Battiri, has been awarded
the "Constance A. Murray Graduate Student Scholarship 2023"



On September 29th, 2023, Vinolia Battiri, the ACM-W NJIT Former Vice President for Master's Students, was honored by NJIT's First Lady, Gina Lim, with "The Committee on Women's Leadership" and "The Women's Employee Resource Group" for winning the Constance A. Murray Graduate Student Scholarship 2023 at the Murray Center for Women in Technology. Nancy Steffen-Fluhr, the Director, announced the scholarship winners during the "Welcome Brunch" event. The committee leadership discussed various topics such as empowering women, finding your voice, and demonstrating how well women can excel in their roles.
